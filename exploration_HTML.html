What is the structure of your dataset?¶

Data set has more than 174k bike sharing ride data covering the greater San Francisco Bay in 2019, Data set has lot of data like:

  • Ride ID
  • Start and End station
  • Start and End time
  • Member age and Gender

Plus I added the following feautre :¶

  • Member age
  • Start and end day and hour

What is/are the main feature(s) of interest in your dataset?¶

I will investigate what the most visited station , peak days and hour .

What features in the dataset do you think will help support your investigation into your feature(s) of interest?¶

I think the following feature will help me:

  • trip duration
  • start and end day and hour
  • Member gender and age
  • User type
